Architecture

This was the first model I built in high school from matt boards. It was based off of an architectural drawing of a staircase.

This box was inspired by the Guggenheim Museum in NYC. I took notable patterns from the building and carved it on each side of the box.

This is the horizontal view of the Guggenheim Box. The top was inspired by the atrium panels in the museum and the sides were inspired by the facade of the museum.

The model was supposed to be a cabinet to hold stationary. However, I made it too small.

This balsa wood bridge is a model of a camel bridge. It commonly used to cross small rivers or streams.
